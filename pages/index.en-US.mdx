## Features

Selendra is a **multichains** _interoperable_ `Nominated Proof-of-Stake` system for developing and running `Substrate-based` and `EVM compatible` blockchain applications.

- On-chain tokens exchange
- Multi-assets 
- Multi-chain token utilities
- Built-in Smart Contract (EVM & Wasm)
- Bridge to EVM chains and Substrate-based networks
- 1000+ TPS on one chain
- Deflationary via fees burns

And a lot [more on Docs](/docs/getting-started).
